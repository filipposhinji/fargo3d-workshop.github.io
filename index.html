When and where

The workshop is going to take place at the UAI building in Pdte Errazuriz 3485 in Las Condes, Santiago de Chile, from January 16 to January 19. The building is within walking distance of the Metro stations Tobalaba, El Golf and Cristóbal Colón. The sessions will start at 9 am and finish at 5 pm. Lunch will be provided.

About

This workshop is dedicated to students and young researchers interested in the topic of hydrodynamical simulations in the context of protoplanetary disks. The workshop aims to deepen the understanding of hydrodynamical processes in numerics, by combining sets of theoretical lectures with sessions of hands-on application.
